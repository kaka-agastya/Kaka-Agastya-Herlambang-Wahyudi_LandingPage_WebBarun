About Us

We specialize in designing and producing ASV (Autonomous Surface Vehicle), FERC (Fuel Engine Remote Control) and RC (Remote Control) boats. Established by ITS Robotics, Metic Club from Marine Engineering, and Hydromodelling Club from Naval Architecture, Barunastra ITS has a strong commitment to help foster the worldâ€™s maritime technology advancement.



Our Vision

Developing unmanned vessels to foster the world's maritime technology advancement

Our Division

Electronics & Programming

The ultimate aim of this division is to deliver high-quality, reliable, and cost-efficient electronic devices and software programs that align with the teams's goals and objectives

Mechanics

The division specializes in designing and producing the hull of the roboboat. They create a robust and efficient hull and customized parts. The end goal is to deliver a high-quality roboboat that meets requirements and exceeds expectations

Non-Technical

The Non-Technical division is responsible for a range of functions that support the overall operation and success of the project. This division's expertise includes project management, finance, marketing, and social branding
